What is this?

This is a silly website made to enhance spooky stories.

There are a variety of ambiences that may be played in the background of your story. You can select 'scream' to harness the power of a spooky scream.

I have produced all of these ambiances myself, carefully looping the audio for infinite playback. I have borrowed bits and pieces from online copyright free resources and supply them for any use as long as you do not use them in any copyrighted works.

May the world feel a little spookier for all of us. -B
